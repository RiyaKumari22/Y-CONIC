Engagement

The Yconic platform was successfully piloted in four schools, engaging 160 students, their families, and 12 teachers. Designed for inclusion, it supports both online and offline participation to bridge the digital divide. Learning outcomes and student campaigns were carefully evaluated. Based on these insights, the platform is being refined and scaled for open-source dissemination, enabling broader global adoption.

Global Roll-Out Vision

Beginning in September 2025, the Yconic platform will enter a roll-out phase targeting onboarding schools from countries in Asia, including India, and other regions in the Global South. This strategic expansion reflects Yconic‚Äôs commitment to inclusivity, ensuring that digitally underrepresented regions and youth voices are integral to the energy transition dialogue.

The roll-out phase, beginning in September 2025, marks a major milestone in scaling the Y-CONIC platform beyond its pilot stage. The initial focus is on onboarding schools across India and other parts of the Global South, including regions in Asia (e.g., Bangladesh, Indonesia), Sub-Saharan Africa, and Latin America

üìú Long-Term Vision & Policy Impact

Y-CONIC's long-term vision extends beyond education‚Äîit aims to become a catalyst for policy change and civic participation. As the platform grows, it will generate a robust body of youth-driven insights and engagement data that can inform national and international energy and climate policy. Key goals include:

  • Producing evidence-based research on energy literacy and digital inclusion
  • Supporting open-source educational tools for wide-scale dissemination
  • Empowering youth as advocates and stakeholders in local and national energy policy
  • Collaborating with policymakers, academic institutions, and think tanks to ensure youth voices shape the just energy transition


By integrating education, AI-driven analysis, and youth leadership, Y-CONIC aims to drive meaningful policy action while building a global network of informed, empowered climate champions.
