Y-CONIC: Youth for Climate, Clean Energy & Digital Inclusion

Empowering the next generation of changemakers

About Us

Yconic is a dynamic, wiki-style interactive platform created to inspire and equip youth aged 12-16 to take leadership roles in the global movement for climate justice, clean energy, and digital inclusion. Backed by the European Commission through Erasmus+ (France), Yconic tackles the three interconnected challenges shaping our world: climate change, the shift to clean energy, and digital transformation.

Our Mission

Yconic's mission is to inspire and prepare the next generation to take local action and participate globally, bridging critical divides in climate, energy, and digital access. By fostering awareness, creativity, and collaboration, Yconic aims to cultivate informed, responsible youth leaders who can drive sustainable change within their communities and beyond.

Background

Supported by the European Commission through Erasmus+ (France), Yconic responds to three powerful forces reshaping today's world: Climate Change The Clean Energy Transition Digitalisation These interconnected challenges demand innovative, inclusive solutions—and Yconic serves as a dynamic space where young minds can explore, contribute, and collaborate. With engaging, peer-driven content and participatory tools, the platform fosters not only knowledge-sharing but also a sense of global citizenship and responsibility.

Core Objectives

Climate & Energy Literacy

Educating youth on the science of climate change and the importance of transitioning to clean, renewable energy sources.

Youth Empowerment

Providing platforms, training, and mentorship to help young people become leaders in sustainability and digital advocacy.

Digital Inclusion

Bridging the digital divide by enhancing access to technology and digital skills, especially in underserved communities.

Communication Campaigns

Running engaging and informative campaigns to raise awareness on climate issues and mobilize community action.

AI/ML-Powered Insights

Leveraging artificial intelligence and machine learning to personalize learning and track engagement outcomes.

FSR Global's Contribution

FSR Global plays a pivotal role in shaping the YCONIC platform by spearheading the development of the Energy Literacy Quiz and contributing to the creation of engaging, educational content. Drawing on its extensive network of over 300 academic and industry experts across Asia, Africa, and Latin America, FSR Global ensures that the material is both relevant and impactful.


In collaboration with other partners, FSR Global is also advancing the use of Natural Language Processing (NLP) to analyze student-generated content and engagement on the YCONIC platform. This research offers valuable insights into student perspectives on energy poverty and digital inequality. The findings support academic /publications and contribute directly to evidence-based policy recommendations.


By integrating AI technologies and empowering youth participation, YCONIC—through FSR Global’s leadership—illustrates the transformative potential of digital tools in raising awareness, fostering advocacy, and driving meaningful policy change for a more equitable and sustainable world.
